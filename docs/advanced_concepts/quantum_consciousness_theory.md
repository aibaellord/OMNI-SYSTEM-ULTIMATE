### Orch-OR Theory Deep Dive

The Orchestrated Objective Reduction (Orch-OR) theory, proposed by Roger Penrose and Stuart Hameroff, posits that consciousness emerges from quantum computations in brain microtubules.

#### Mathematical Formulation
The theory combines quantum mechanics with general relativity:

\[ |\psi\rangle_{microtubule} = \sum_n c_n |tubulin_n\rangle \]

Objective reduction occurs when:

\[ \Delta t \approx \frac{\hbar}{E_{grav}} \]

Where \( E_{grav} \) is the gravitational self-energy difference.

#### Microtubule Quantum Computing
Microtubules act as quantum processors with tubulin dimers in superposition:

- **Qubit Representation**: Each dimer as a qubit
- **Entanglement**: Between adjacent dimers
- **Decoherence**: Gravitational collapse causing consciousness
